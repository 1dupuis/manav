About Manav

Manav is a passionate sports photographer with over a decade of experience capturing the intensity, grace, and drama of athletic performances across various disciplines. His unique eye for detail and commitment to storytelling through images have made him a sought-after professional in the world of sports photography.

With a background in both photography and sports, Manav brings a deep understanding of athletic movements and game dynamics to his work, allowing him to anticipate and capture those split-second moments that define sporting excellence.
